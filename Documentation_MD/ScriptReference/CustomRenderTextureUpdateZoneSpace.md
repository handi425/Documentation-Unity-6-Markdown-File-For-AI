### Description

Space in which coordinates are provided for Update Zones.

Additional resources: [CustomRenderTexture](CustomRenderTexture.html).

### Properties

[Normalized](CustomRenderTextureUpdateZoneSpace.Normalized.html)| Coordinates
are normalized. (0, 0) is top left and (1, 1) is bottom right.  
---|---  
[Pixel](CustomRenderTextureUpdateZoneSpace.Pixel.html)| Coordinates are
expressed in pixels. (0, 0) is top left (width, height) is bottom right.
