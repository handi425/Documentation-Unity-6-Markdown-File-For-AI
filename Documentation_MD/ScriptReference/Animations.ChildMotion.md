### Description

Structure that represents a motion in the context of its parent blend tree.

### Properties

[cycleOffset](Animations.ChildMotion-cycleOffset.html)| Normalized time offset
of the child.  
---|---  
[directBlendParameter](Animations.ChildMotion-directBlendParameter.html)| The
parameter used by the child when used in a BlendTree of type
BlendTreeType.Direct.  
[mirror](Animations.ChildMotion-mirror.html)| Mirror of the child.  
[motion](Animations.ChildMotion-motion.html)| The motion itself.  
[position](Animations.ChildMotion-position.html)| The position of the child.
Used in 2D blend trees.  
[threshold](Animations.ChildMotion-threshold.html)| The threshold of the
child. Used in 1D blend trees.  
[timeScale](Animations.ChildMotion-timeScale.html)| The relative speed of the
child.
