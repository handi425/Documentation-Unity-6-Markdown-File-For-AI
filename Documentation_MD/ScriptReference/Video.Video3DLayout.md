### Description

Types of 3D content layout within a video.

### Properties

[No3D](Video.Video3DLayout.No3D.html)| Video does not have any 3D content.  
---|---  
[SideBySide3D](Video.Video3DLayout.SideBySide3D.html)| Video contains 3D
content where the left eye occupies the left half and right eye occupies the
right half of video frames.  
[OverUnder3D](Video.Video3DLayout.OverUnder3D.html)| Video contains 3D content
where the left eye occupies the upper half and right eye occupies the lower
half of video frames.
