public float normalImpulse;

### Description

Gets the impulse applied at the contact point along the
[ContactPoint2D.normal](ContactPoint2D-normal.html).

The impulse is applied to both the [rigidbody](ContactPoint2D-rigidbody.html)
and the [otherRigidbody](ContactPoint2D-otherRigidbody.html) at the
[point](ContactPoint2D-point.html) along the
[normal](ContactPoint2D-normal.html) and the inverse
[normal](ContactPoint2D-normal.html).
