### Description

Ultra wide angle camera. These devices have a shorter focal length than a
wide-angle camera.

On iOS devices, this value is reported directly by the hardware.  
  
On Android devices, this value is reported for cameras with a calculated
Equivalent Focal Length of less than 20mm.  
  
Additional resources: [WebCamDevice.kind](WebCamDevice-kind.html).
