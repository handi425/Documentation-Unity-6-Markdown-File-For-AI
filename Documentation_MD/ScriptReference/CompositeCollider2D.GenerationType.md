### Description

Specifies when to generate the Composite Collider geometry.

Controls when the Composite Collider geometry is updated when a Collider used
by a Composite Collider changes.  
  
Additional resources:
[CompositeCollider2D.generationType](CompositeCollider2D-generationType.html).

### Properties

[Synchronous](CompositeCollider2D.GenerationType.Synchronous.html)| Sets the
Composite Collider geometry to update synchronously immediately when a
Collider used by the Composite Collider changes.  
---|---  
[Manual](CompositeCollider2D.GenerationType.Manual.html)| Sets the Composite
Collider geometry to not automatically update when a Collider used by the
Composite Collider changes.
